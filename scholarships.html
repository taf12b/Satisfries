Scholarship Opportunities

The Army is always changing, and so is its ability to hand out ROTC scholarships. Scholarships are won by hard-working, physically and mentally fit individuals. Contact our enrollment officer CPT Bob Lovins (Ret.) by phone: (850) 644-1016 or or by email: rlovins@fsu.edu to learn more about scholarship and contracting.
