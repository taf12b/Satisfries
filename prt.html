Physical Readiness Training (PRT)

Physical Readiness Training (PRT) is the main method we employ to keep our cadets in shape and able to pass the Army Physical Fitness test.

The Army Physical Fitness Test (APFT) is comprised of three events; pushups, situps and 2 mile run. To pass the APFT you must score at least 60 points in each event. To download the Army Physical Training guide, click on the following link.
